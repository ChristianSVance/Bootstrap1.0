Lessons

Surf & Travel Camp - Surf Coaching day trip

The beautiful beach of Sandbridge are the ideal place to learn to surf or hone your surf skills, and this full-day surf experience is suitable for all levels. Meet your guide at 10am and transfer to the beach, where you will enjoy a 2-hour surf lesson tailored to your level, including complete beginners.

Surf in Ocian City, with local surf instructor

Over the years, we have had the privilege of meeting, teaching, learning from, and just experiencing life with some truly outstanding people. Our guests are a constant source of inspiration and motivation for us. We are truly thankful to each and every one of our guests, as they give us the drive to always stay at the cutting edge of what we do.

Beginner surf in Popano beach

The Atlantic coast resort of Pompano Beach is a popular choice for surfers of all levels, and this beginner class will set you on the way to surfing your first waves. Learn the basics under the guidance of a professional surf instructor and enjoy plenty of time to get in the water and practice your newfound skills during a 2-hour class.

Virgina Beach 2 Hour Surf Experience

Enjoy fun and safe surfing lessons with warm-up, theory and practice. Learn or improve your surfing at Virgina Beach where the best waves are! The best surfing experience from local and certified surf instructors. Let's enjoy our time together!
